Welcome to K.A.R.E Rescue

Kenine Animal Rescue And Empaths Society.

Welcome to Kare Rescue Center, where compassion meets action. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need, providing them with a second chance at life. We are dedicated to promoting animal welfare through education and community outreach. With a team of passionate volunteers and experts, we strive to create a safe and loving environment for every animal that comes through our doors. Join us in making a difference, one paw at a time.
